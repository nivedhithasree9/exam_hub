Code Of Conduct
===============

Our Pledge
----------

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project environment for everyone.

Expected Behavior
-----------------

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.
- Keep feedback specific and focused on the work.
- Respect different backgrounds, experience levels, and communication styles.

Unacceptable Behavior
---------------------

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated disruptive behavior after being asked to stop

Enforcement
-----------

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who violate
this code of conduct.
